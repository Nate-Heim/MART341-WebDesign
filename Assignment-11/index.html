Lord of the Rings

lord of the rings is an epic high fantasy novel written by english author and scholar J.R.R. Tolkien.
The story began as a sequel to tolkien's 1937 fantasy novel the hobbit, but eventually developed into a much larger work. written in stages between 1937 and 1949,
the lord of the rings is one of the best-selling novels ever written, with over 150 million copies sold. It didn't take long for the story to be adapted into a movie series.
Soon after this, the story was adapted into many different video game series under this canon.

The first game in the series was released in 1982, and was a text-based adventure game titled The Hobbit: A Software Adventure
Here is a link to a retro version of the game: The Hobbit: A Software Adventure
The game was released on many different platforms, including the ZX Spectrum, Commodore 64, Apple II, MS-DOS, and many more.

Thousands of people have fallen in loive with this series, and it has become a staple in the fantasy genre.
The story has been adapted into many different forms of media, including movies, video games, and even a musical.
Many people have been inspired by this series, and have created their own stories based on the lore of the series.
You can see some similarities in the world of Dungeons and Dragons, and even in the world of Minecraft.(though I just may be seeing things here)
