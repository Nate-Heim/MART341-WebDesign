About Dragons

Dragons are legendary creatures that appear in the folklore of many cultures around the world. These majestic beings are often depicted as large, serpentine creatures with wings and the ability to breathe fire.

Dragon Haven is dedicated to exploring the rich history and mythology of dragons. Discover the fascinating stories, learn about different dragon species, and delve into the magical world where these mythical beings exist.

Remember Dragons themselves are dangerous in mythology, so while they are phenomenal creatures, they are not to be trifled with.
