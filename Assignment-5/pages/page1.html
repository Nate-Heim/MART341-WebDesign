Patrick Rothfuss

Patrick Rothfuss is a renowned American fantasy author known for his critically acclaimed debut novel, "The Name of the Wind," published in 2007.
Born on June 6, 1973, in Madison, Wisconsin, Rothfuss gained widespread recognition for his meticulous world-building and lyrical prose, earning him numerous awards
and a devoted fanbase. He is also known for his philanthropic work through his charity organization, Worldbuilders, which raises funds for various global causes.
